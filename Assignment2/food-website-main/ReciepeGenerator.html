Food Recipe

Paneer Tikka

Marinate paneer cubes in yogurt and spices, then grill until golden and charred. Serve hot with lemon wedges and mint chutney.

Allo Tikki

Mash boiled potatoes with spices, shape into patties, and fry until crispy. Serve hot with chutneys and toppings of your choice.

Kaju Kari

To make Kaju Kari, roast cashews until golden. Toss with spices like turmeric, chili powder, and salt for a flavorful snack.

Samosa

For Samosas, fill pastry dough with a mixture of spiced potatoes, peas, and herbs. Deep-fry until golden and crispy.

Bhaji Pav

Cook mixed vegetables with spices and herbs, serve with buttered pav buns for a delicious Bhaji Pav.

Panipuri

Mix tangy tamarind water with mashed potato and chickpeas, fill crispy puris for a refreshing Panipuri delight.
